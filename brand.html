Rejouva Brand Guidelines

The visual and verbal system behind Rejouva France Patches — built for clinical authority, not beauty fluff.

01 · Brand Essence

Clinical authority, French restraint.

Positioning

Medical-grade periorbital restoration for the skeptical, time-poor executive. We sell mechanism and evidence — never "look younger."

Personality

Precise, credible, calm. A dermatology brief written by someone with impeccable taste. Confident without hype.

Feeling

Quiet relief and control. The reassurance of evidence. Porcelain calm with a single warm, human rose.

06 · Imagery & Motion

Treatment.

Every image gets a dark gradient from the bottom + a rose mix-blend-multiply wash. Cohesive, premium, never raw stock.

Motion principles

  • · Animate transform & opacity only — never transition-all
  • · Spring easing cubic-bezier(.16,1,.3,1)
  • · Scroll-reveal: fade + 22px rise, staggered
  • · Respect prefers-reduced-motion
  • · Subtle grain overlay on every section for depth

07 · Voice & Tone

How Rejouva speaks.

She is highly educated and cynical of beauty marketing. Lead with mechanism and numbers; respect her intelligence. Name the problem precisely, then prove the solution.

Say this

  • · "Mathematically too big to cross the 500-Dalton barrier."
  • · ">95% purified · 100–243 Da · clinically evaluated."
  • · "Drains pooled morning fluid. Hands-free."
  • · Specific, mechanistic, measured.

Never this

  • · "Look decades younger instantly!"
  • · "Miracle · secret · anti-aging holy grail"
  • · Celebrity endorsements, hype, exclamation spam
  • · Vague "nourishes & revitalizes" filler.
