    Because it is structurally different, not just marketed differently. Every cream you tried has the same problem: molecules too large to enter your skin.     Is PDRN (salmon DNA) actually safe? Will I react?

    Rejouva's PDRN is purified at >95% purity — completely free of proteins and peptides that trigger immune responses. Salmon DNA is approximately 95% homologous to human DNA, so human cells recognize it as familiar rather than foreign. This formula contains no retinoids, no fragrances, no Vitamin C derivatives, no allergens. It was designed specifically for the thin, reactive skin of the periorbital zone.

    No. Rejouva uses a clinical-grade airtight sealing mechanism specifically designed to prevent the volatile solvent evaporation that ruins most premium patch jars. Many users report the patches on day 14 feel identical to day 1. If yours ever dry out, contact us — we'll replace the jar, full stop.

    Will this pill or leave residue under my concealer?

    No. The active ingredients are all engineered below 243 Da — they absorb completely into the skin. There is no high-molecular-weight polymer film remaining on the surface.     My eye bags are genetic. Can anything actually help?

Dark shadows come from dilated capillaries showing through thin skin — again, mechanical and vascular, not purely structural. Rejouva addresses both. Many users with lifelong "genetic" eye bags see meaningful daily improvement, even without changing the underlying bone structure.
